# ANSI SLAS 1-2004 (R2012) — Footprint Dimensions

Source: `ANSI_SLAS_1-2004_FootprintDimensions.pdf`

## Dimensions

| Parameter | Nominal | Tolerance (corners) | Tolerance (along side) |
|-----------|---------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Length    | 127.76 mm | ± 0.25 mm | ± 0.5 mm |
| Width     | 85.48 mm  | ± 0.25 mm | ± 0.5 mm |
| Corner radius | 3.18 mm | ± 1.6 mm | — |

## Notes

- Corner tolerance zone: measured within 12.7 mm (0.5000 in) of outside corners.
- 4× corner radii: R 3.18 ± 1.6 mm (0.1252 ± 0.0630 in).
- Footprint must be continuous and uninterrupted around the base of the plate.
- Geometry shown is illustrative only — no preferred construction implied.
- Dimensions do not include draft.
- Reference datum: bottom plane (Datum A), long side (Datum B/D-E), short side (Datum C/B-C).
- All dims at 20 °C.

## Datum scheme (from Annex A)

- Datum A = bottom face of plate
- Datum B = long side face
- Datum C = short side face
- Composite datums (D-E, B-C) used for tight corner zones
