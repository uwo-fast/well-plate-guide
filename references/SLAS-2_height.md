# ANSI SLAS 2-2004 (R2012) — Height Dimensions

Source: `ANSI_SLAS_2-2004_HeightDimensions.pdf`

## Two compliance options

Plates conform to either 4.1 (height with clearance) or 4.2 (typical height).
Must state which.

## Dimensions (both options share these)

| Parameter | Value | Tolerance |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Plate height (to perimeter well max protrusion) | 14.35 mm | ± 0.25 mm |
| Overall plate height (to max protrusion of plate) | 14.35 mm | ± 0.76 mm |
| Max projection above top-stacking surface | 0.76 mm | — |
| Top surface parallelism to resting plane | — | within 0.76 mm |

## Section 4.1 only: External clearance

- Min clearance from Datum A to bottom external surface of wells: **1 mm** (area of wells only).

## Key dims from figure

- Height: 14.35 mm from Datum A (resting plane) — see Note A.
- Top projection zone: 0.00/0.76 mm above stacking surface.
- Area "K" (well zone): tighter ± 0.25 mm tolerance applies here.
- Dimension "D": 4× corner features shown in plan view (corner chamfer zones).
- 1 mm min clearance below wells (Note B: applies in well area only).

## Notes

- Datum A = resting plane (bottom of plate).
- Tolerances do not include draft.
- All dims at 20 °C.
