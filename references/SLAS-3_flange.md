# ANSI SLAS 3-2004 (R2012) — Bottom Outside Flange Dimensions

Source: `ANSI_SLAS_3-2004_BottomOutsideFlangeDimensions.pdf`

## Overview

Defines the external skirt/flange that hangs below the plate's resting plane (Datum A).
Five compliance options — plates must state which they meet.

## Flange height options

| Section | Style | Height | Tolerance |
|---------|-------|--------|-----------|
| 4.1 | Short | 2.41 mm | ± 0.38 mm |
| 4.2 | Medium | 6.10 mm | ± 0.38 mm |
| 4.3 | Tall | 7.62 mm | ± 0.38 mm |
| 4.4 | Short with interruptions | 2.41 mm | ± 0.38 mm |
| 4.5 | Dual (short sides / long sides) | 2.41 / 7.62 mm | ± 0.38 mm each |

## Common to all options

- **Flange width** (at top of flange): min 1.27 mm
- **Chamfers**: optional; if present, must not include the flange.
- Sections 4.1–4.3: flange height must be same on all four sides.

## Section 4.4 — Interruptions

- One interruption/projection allowed per long side, centered.
- Each edge of interruption: min 47.8 mm from nearest plate edge.
- Max interruption width: 32.16 mm (derived: 127.76 − 2×47.8 = 32.16)
- Max flange height at interruption: 6.85 mm

## Section 4.5 — Dual heights

- Short sides: 2.41 ± 0.38 mm
- Long sides: 7.62 ± 0.38 mm

## From Figure 1

- Flange width min: 1.27 mm (0.0500 in), shown with exaggerated draft.
- Measured from Datum A (bottom resting plane) upward to top of flange.
- Draft angle shown but not dimensioned (illustrative only).

## Notes

- All dims at 20 °C.
- Tolerances do not include draft.
